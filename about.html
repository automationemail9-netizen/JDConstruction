About Jarek Datta

Jarek Datta is a multi‑skilled construction worker with a reputation for reliability, precision, and honest communication. He works across a wide range of domestic and small commercial projects, taking responsibility for everything from the first measurement to the final clean‑up.

Whether you need a single bathroom refurbished or a full property upgrade, Jarek approaches every job with the same level of care and professionalism.

How Jarek Works

1. Initial Conversation

Jarek listens carefully to what you want—whether it’s a simple upgrade or a full redesign—and asks practical questions about budget, timing, and how you use the space.

2. Site Visit & Measurements

He visits the property, takes accurate measurements, checks existing plumbing and electrics, and identifies any potential issues before work begins.

3. Clear Plan & Quote

You receive a clear breakdown of the work, materials, and estimated timeline. Jarek explains what’s involved in plain language so there are no surprises.

4. Careful Execution

Work is carried out with respect for your home: surfaces are protected, dust is managed, and each stage is checked before moving on.

5. Finishing & Clean‑Up

Once the job is complete, fixtures are tested, finishes are inspected, and the area is cleaned so it’s ready to use immediately.

Professional Standards

  • Respectful, punctual, and straightforward communication
  • Work carried out to agreed specifications and timelines
  • Focus on durability, not just appearance
  • Use of appropriate materials for each environment (wet rooms, outdoor spaces, etc.)
  • Photos and updates provided throughout longer projects
